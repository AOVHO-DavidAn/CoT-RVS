# T-ReasonVOS Benchmark

To evaluate the performance of reasoning segmentation in temporally sensitive cases, we introduce a new benchmark T-ReasonVOS. This dataset is a subset of [ReasonVOS](https://github.com/showlab/VideoLISA/blob/main/BENCHMARK.md). It consists of 180 video-query pairs that correpond to 45 videos, including 8 from [MeViS](https://github.com/henghuiding/MeViS) (released under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 License), 16 from [BURST](https://github.com/Ali2500/BURST-benchmark) (released under MIT License), 18 from [MOSE](https://github.com/henghuiding/MOSE-api) (released under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 License), and 3 from [VIPSeg](https://github.com/VIPSeg-Dataset/VIPSeg-Dataset) (released under non-commercial research purpose only).

## Usage

1. Please refer to [ReasonVOS](https://github.com/showlab/VideoLISA/blob/main/BENCHMARK.md) to download the dataset. 
2. Replace `meta_expressions.json` with our `meta_expressions_temp.json` for T-ReasonVOS usage.
